The complete espresso practice

An espresso shot timer, a coffee bean tracker, and an AI brewing partner — built for the people who take their craft seriously.

Espresso Shot Timer

A full-screen espresso shot timer guides you through pre-extraction, the sweet window, and the moment to stop. The extraction arc sweeps in real time and haptic feedback signals the perfect pull.

AI Coffee Recipes

Scan any coffee bag and Extraction reads the label — roaster, origin, process, variety — and generates a personalised AI coffee recipe tuned to your specific equipment.

Coffee Bean Tracker

Track every bag from wishlist to archive with a built-in coffee freshness tracker. Peak alerts fire when a bean enters its sweet window. Never brew a past-peak coffee again.

Grinder Settings, Solved

157 grinders backfilled with their native scale and recommended settings per brew method. The dial opens with the sweet-spot arc already lit for your specific grinder.

Acaia Scale Integration

Pair an Acaia Lunar, Pearl, or Pyxis and the brew runs itself — tare, timer, first-drip, yield, and pour curve all automatic. The world's most precise brew scale, fully integrated.

AI Coaching

A weekly AI coaching insight generated from your shot history — specific observations about your technique, your coffees, and the one change most likely to improve your next brew.
